### PI — Becca Napolitano
Overall grant direction, NSF reporting, budget oversight. Technical leads: telemetry framework (D1), Composable Courseware integration (D2), Data Translation Framework (D7). Primary writer for publications targeting RQ2. Manages coordination with CAMEL cross-network activities.

### Co-PI — James Yao
Directs the research program and science of learning component. Leads data collection protocol design, annotation reliability (D6), proof-of-concept classifier (D8). Primary writer for publications targeting RQ1.

### Co-PI — Wesley Reinhart
Data originator for materials science domain. Provides 2DCC-MIP AFM datasets (open-access on Penn State ScholarSphere). Architects the Composable Courseware layer (YAML-manifest compiler). Ensures FAIR compliance for materials data.

### Co-PI — Wangda Zuo
Data originator for cyber-physical / building energy systems domain. Provides campus-scale district cooling network and university data center cooling datasets. Ensures technical accuracy of energy infrastructure curriculum context.

Mayne leads teacher PD curriculum (summer institutes) and standards alignment. Cesare tunes complexity dials for classroom appropriateness.
